Information Systems

The Information Systems study program emphasizes how to utilize information and communication technology (ICT) to solve problems faced by a business or organization and can provide an evaluation of the strategic value of the use of ICT in achieving organizational goals.

Visual Communication Design

Visual Communication Design is a branch of the design sciences that studies how to deliver messages using creative expressions to achieve certain goals, such as providing information or persuasion (as in advertising). The techniques and media that use visual elements to convey the message are also studied.

Interior Design

The Interior Design Study Program aims to educate students to create a positive influence through both the creative process and the engineering process. On how to produce technical design in a space based on the principles of robustness, usability, and beauty.
